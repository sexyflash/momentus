All app settings and timer data are stored locally on your device. We do not store any user data on our servers.

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App can be used without providing any personal information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data. As all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features.
